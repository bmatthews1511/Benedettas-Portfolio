Sabrina Carpenter

The girl who reinvented pop music

Here's a time line of Sabrina Carpenter's career

  • 1999 - Sabrina was born in Quarker, Pennsylvania.
  • 2011 - Sabrina landed her first acting role on NBC's Law & Order: Special Victim's Unit.
  • 2012 Sabrina landed a recurring part on the Goodwin Games, a sitcom produced by Fox. She also acted in Horns that would be released the next year, and voiced "Princess Vivian" in Sofia the First.
  • 2013 - Sabrina played Maya Heart in Disney Channel's series, Girl Meets World.
  • 2014 - She joined Hollywood Records, a record label owned by Disney. Sabrina released her debut single that was titled "Can't Blame a Girl for Trying." She then released her debut EP a month later.
  • 2015 - Sabrina released the lead single for her first album, "Eyes Wide Open." The single was titled "We'll be the Stars." The album charted at 43 on the Billboard 200.
  • 2016 - She released another single named "Smoke and Fire" and performed this at the 2016 Radio Disney Music Awards. A few months later, she headlined the Bethlehem's Musikfest festival.
  • 2017 - Sabrina was featured on The Vamps's song "Hands" with Andi mack. She then released another single called "Why" that charted at number 21 on the Billboard Bubbling Under Hot 100. She then opened for Ariana Grande on her Dangerous Woman Tour.
  • 2018 - Sabrina Carpenter was featured in Nylon magazine. The article was titled "25 Gen Z'ers changing the World." Her third album titled Singular: Act I was released as well.
  • 2019 - Sabrina went on her third concert tour called the "Singular Tour." She then starred in the film The Short History of the Long Road that premiered at the Tribeca Film Festival and received praise by critics. She then released her fourth album, Singular: Act II
  • 2020 - Sabrina released an R&B single Honeymoon Fades. She then landed a role on Broadway for Mean Girls. Sabrina was then on Forbes' 30 under 30 list.
  • 2021 - Sabrina signed with a new record label "Universal Music Group's Island Records." She then appeared in the Savage X Fenty Show in September. She then released a single for her upcoming fifth album, the single called "Skinny Dipping."
  • 2022 - Sabrina released her best-selling album, "Emails I Can't Send." This album peaked at 23 on the Billboard 200. She then began on the Emails I Can't Send Tour
  • 2023 - Sabrina opened up for Taylor Swift on the Eras Tour for some of the shows in Latin America, Australia and Singapore. She then released a Christmas-themed EP titled "Fruitcake" in November.
